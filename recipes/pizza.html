Pizza

Description

Authentic Neapolitan-style pizza dough recipe with the signature bubbly char. This overnight dough is made with Type 00 flour, water, salt, and yeast and can be refrigerated for up to 5 days.

Ingrediens

  • 665gr flour
  • 1/8 tsp instant yeast
  • Water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

We will start with dough.

  1. Put the flour in a large bowl add some salt and the yeast on it mix it dry
  2. Start adding the 450gr water slowly in the bowl while stirring
  3. After all mixed take your dough on the table and start kneading with hands
  4. After finishing kneading put the dough again in the bowl and cover it with cloths. Be carreful that the temperatur of the room is above 20 celcius degree is.
  5. Wait until the dough rise and fill the bowl
  6. Take 100gr dough and give it the shape of pizza
  7. Put the tomato sauce on it and some mozeralle
  8. Bake for 15-20min in 180 degree
  9. Put some fresh basilage leafs before serving
